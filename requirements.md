# Grameen AI Sahayak

## Problem
Millions of rural citizens in India struggle to access government schemes and digital services due to language barriers, low digital literacy, and complex processes.

## Solution
Grameen AI Sahayak is a multilingual AI-powered assistant that helps rural users access government services using voice and simple text interaction.

## Key Features
- Voice-based interaction in Hindi and regional languages
- Government scheme discovery
- Eligibility checker
- Step-by-step form assistance
- Offline-friendly support

## Target Users
- Farmers
- Rural citizens
- Senior citizens
- Low literacy populations

## Impact
Promotes digital inclusion and empowers rural communities.
